What is AreaPilot?
   Hate it when you go to an event and your friends are no where to be found? AreaPilot is a way to solve this problem. With AreaPilot you can post events you have heard about and allows your community of friends to vote on their favorites and mark the events they plan on attending.

How does AreaPilot work?
   With AreaPilot you can follow your friends profiles and using the Facebook Like and Twitter Share buttons you can let all your friends know where you are going to be this Friday. Once you and your friends vote on an event; the favorites go to the top of the list. With AreaPilot you get to know how many of your friends are attending and the time and place of the event to ensure you and your friends are going to the same one. Use AreaPilot to ensure that you and your friends always end up at the right event
